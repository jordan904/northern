About Northern Heat Pumps

Northern Heat Pumps is a locally owned HVAC company proudly serving Nova Scotia's North Shore. We specialize in Panasonic heat pump systems — delivering energy-efficient heating and cooling solutions to homeowners across Cumberland, Colchester, and Pictou counties.

As a Kerr Controls and Efficiency Nova Scotia partner, we help our customers access the best rebate programs available — saving up to $15,000 on new installations. Our commitment goes beyond the install: we're your neighbours, and we stand behind every job with a 10-year warranty on all heat pump systems.

Whether you're switching from oil, upgrading an older system, or building new, we'll find the right solution for your home and budget.
